Privacy Policy for Kvault

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our K‑pop collection cataloging application (the "App"). The App is designed to help users catalog, organize, and manage their personal K‑pop collections, including photocards, albums, and related items.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We may collect limited personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Email address (for account creation, login, or support)
  • Username or display name
  • Subscription or purchase status (if applicable)

We do not require real names.

b. Collection & Content Data

The App stores information you choose to add, including:

  • K‑pop group and artist names
  • Photocard details (member name, era, album, version)
  • Images you upload of your collection
  • Notes, tags, and custom fields you create

This data is used solely to provide the App's cataloging features.

c. Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect limited technical data such as:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • App usage data (features used, crashes, performance)
  • Anonymous identifiers

This information is used to improve app performance and stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain App functionality
  • Sync and back up your collection data
  • Enable premium or subscription features (if applicable)
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Respond to user support requests
  • Communicate important updates (only when necessary)

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Images and Media

Any images you upload (such as photocards or albums) are:

  • Stored for your personal use within the App
  • Not publicly visible unless you explicitly enable sharing features

You retain ownership of all images you upload.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal or collection data with third parties except:

  • With service providers that help operate the App (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of users or the App

All third‑party services are required to protect your data.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

If the App offers paid features:

  • Payments are processed through third‑party app stores (e.g., Apple App Store, Google Play)
  • We do not store or have access to your payment details
  • Subscription status may be stored to manage feature access

6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure servers and encrypted connections (where applicable)
  • Limited access to user data

However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active.

You may:

  • Delete items from your collection at any time
  • Request account and data deletion through the App or by contacting support

Once deleted, your data will be permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date and notify users of significant changes through the App.
